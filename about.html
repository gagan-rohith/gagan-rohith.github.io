About Me

Hello! My name is Gagan Rohit, and I am a Master's Graduate in Computer Science from the New Jersey Institute of Technology. Two of the things I enjoy most about programming are building cool (and hopefully useful) projects and tackling algorithmic challenges that push me to think creatively and analytically. My primary coding languages are Java, Python, JavaScript, and C#, and I thrive in the process of turning ideas into functional, innovative solutions.

In addition to my technical expertise, I have a deep passion for cricket and music production. Cricket has always been a sport I admire for its blend of strategy, teamwork, and skill, while music-making allows me to explore my creative side in a different way. The intersection of these interests drives me to approach problems with both logical precision and a sense of artistry.
